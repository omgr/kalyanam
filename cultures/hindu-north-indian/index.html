Pre-Wedding Ceremonies

5 rituals

Ceremonies and rituals performed before the wedding day

Roka / Tilak

(रोका / तिलक)

The formal ceremony where both families agree to the match. Tilak is applied to the groom's forehead.

60 days before1h 30m

Sagai / Ring Ceremony

(सगाई)

Exchange of rings between the bride and groom. Gifts and sweets are exchanged between families.

30 days before2h

Sangeet

(संगीत)Optional

A musical night with songs, dances, and performances from both families. DJ and choreographed dances are common.

2 days before5h

Mehndi

(मेहंदी)

Intricate henna designs are applied on the bride's hands and feet. Other women also get mehndi.

1 day before5h

Haldi

(हल्दी)

Turmeric paste is applied to both bride and groom at their respective homes for glowing skin.

1 day before2h

Wedding Day Rituals

7 rituals

The main wedding ceremony and associated rituals

Baraat

(बारात)

Grand procession of the groom to the wedding venue on a decorated horse or car with music and dancing.

Wedding Day2h

Milni

(मिलनी)

Formal meeting of counterparts from both families. They exchange garlands and gifts.

Wedding Day30 min

Jaimala / Varmala

(जयमाला / वरमाला)

Exchange of flower garlands between bride and groom, often made competitive by lifting them.

Wedding Day15 min

Kanyadaan

(कन्यादान)

The bride's father gives his daughter's hand to the groom. Considered the most sacred act.

Wedding Day30 min

Mangal Pheras

(मंगल फेरे)

Four rounds around the sacred fire. Each phera has specific vows for dharma, artha, kama, and moksha.

Wedding Day45 min

Sindoor & Mangalsutra

(सिंदूर & मंगलसूत्र)

Groom applies sindoor (vermillion) to the bride's hair parting and ties the mangalsutra.

Wedding Day15 min

Saptapadi

(सप्तपदी)

Seven steps together, each representing a vow. The marriage is complete after the seventh step.

Wedding Day20 min

Post-Wedding Ceremonies

3 rituals

Ceremonies performed after the wedding

Vidaai

(विदाई)

Emotional farewell of the bride from her parental home. She throws rice over her shoulder.

Wedding Day30 min

Griha Pravesh

(गृह प्रवेश)

Bride enters her new home, kicks a pot of rice with her right foot.

Wedding Day30 min

Reception

(रिसेप्शन)Optional

Grand party hosted by groom's family to introduce the bride to their extended family and friends.

1 day after5h
